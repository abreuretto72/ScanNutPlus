By choosing to use our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. The Personal Information that we collect is used for providing and improving the Service. We will not share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, we may require you to provide certain information, including:

  • Account Data: Name, email, and password entered during registration.
  • Pet Data: Name, breed, age, weight, medical information, and your pet's clinical history.
  • Media (Camera and Microphone): Photos, videos, and audio captured specifically for analysis within the app.
  • Location (GPS): Approximate or precise location data when using the Map or Walk Diary features.

2. Generative AI Processing (Google Gemini)

ScanNut+ actively uses Generative Artificial Intelligence technologies, specifically the Gemini API (provided by Google), to process visual, audio, and text data to generate predictive health and wellness reports for animals and plants.

  • Data Transmission: When you submit a photo, video, or audio file, this file and associated metadata are securely and anonymously transmitted to Google Gemini API servers.
  • Exclusive Purpose: The transmitted data is used exclusively to generate the requested analysis within the app's interface.
  • Disclaimer: All analyses generated by Gemini are for guidance only. They DO NOT replace professional veterinary evaluation, diagnosis, or treatment.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Storage: Records and reports (PDFs) build up the health history on your device and are synced to our secure servers (Google Cloud/Firebase).
  • Data Deletion: Deleting a Pet profile irreversibly erases the entire history of consultations, AI analyses (Gemini), appointments, and associated media for that profile from our active databases.
